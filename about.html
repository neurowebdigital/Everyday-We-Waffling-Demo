Our Story

What started as a passion for perfectly golden waffles quickly turned into one of Cape Town’s happiest dessert spots. From handcrafted Belgian waffles to over-the-top milkshakes, every item on our menu is made fresh and designed to create moments worth remembering.

We believe dessert isn’t just food — it’s an experience. It’s birthday celebrations, date nights, family laughs, and late-night cravings all in one.

“We started Everyday We Waffling with one goal — to create a place where people don’t just eat dessert, but share moments.”
— Founder, Everyday We Waffling
